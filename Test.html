Who We Are

RenewRedo is a Colorado only, grass roots cooperative which actively promotes and integrates a sustainable and holistic approach to home and business ownership and offers a pathway to achieving these goals.

What We Do

Utilizing decades of experience, local vetted professional and highly rated companies, and organizing them within our philosophy, we can offer a path to energy independence with products such as solar electric, solar hot water, heating and cooling, energy management, demand side management, windows, insulation, roofing, battery systems, off grid complete solar solutions, home automation, appliances as well as consulting, project management, licensed general and electrical contracting and various real estate and financing sources.

Mission Statement

We believe that each organization has its specific niche and when offered as a part of a cooperative effort our goals will be achieved in a professional and timely manner. Instead of endless debate and going from contractor to contractor, our philosophy is to offer them under one project.
